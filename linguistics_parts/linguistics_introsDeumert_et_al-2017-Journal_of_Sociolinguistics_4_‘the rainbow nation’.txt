1.  INTRODUCTION.  LIVING-IN-DIVERSITY, BEYOND THE RAINBOW.  In the early 1990s, anti-apartheid stalwart Desmond Tutu coined the metaphor of ‘the rainbow nation’ to describe the ‘miracle’ of South African democracy and the diversity of its people.  The image of the rainbow also evoked, probably unintentionally, a well-known Euro-American symbol of LGBT+ identity and pride.
